# J.C. Penney (jc-penney)
J.C. Penney Company, Inc. (JCPenney) is an American department store chain offering apparel, accessories, beauty, jewelry, footwear, and home goods. The company emerged from Chapter 11 bankruptcy in late 2020 and continues to operate hundreds of department stores across the United States and Puerto Rico.
